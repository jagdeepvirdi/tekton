Our Story

Rooftop Dreams
to Modern Design

Born in 2022 on a Kanpur rooftop. Forged in resin and wood.

Founded 2022

Where the Idea Took Root

Our journey began not in a traditional workshop, but on a rooftop garden in Kanpur — where the contrast of nature and modernity first sparked our imagination. Each sunrise brought new sketches, drawn from the delicate interplay of light and shadow, the textures of natural materials, and a deep desire to create something more than furniture.

In a world where design often feels dictated by trends and mass production, we set out with a simple dream: to blend functionality with artistic expression. To create pieces that don’t merely serve a purpose, but evoke emotions, tell stories, and become a permanent part of the spaces they inhabit.

Drawing from various cultures and design philosophies, we wanted our furniture to transcend mere utility — to embody minimalist aesthetics and the rich, tactile beauty of natural materials, from sustainably sourced wood to hand-poured resin that captures light in ways no paint ever could.

Each piece we craft tells a story of innovation, sustainability, and a commitment to quality that resonates with those who appreciate modern aesthetics.

The Making

How Each Table Comes to Life

01

The Wood

We source dried hardwood slabs — mango, sheesham, teak — chosen for grain character and structural integrity. Each slab is planed, sanded, and prepared over several days before resin ever touches it.

02

The Pour

Resin is mixed by hand in small batches, tinted to the chosen colour, and poured in layers over two to three days. No shortcuts — each layer must fully cure before the next. This is where the piece becomes unmistakably ours.

03

Cure & Finish

After full cure, the surface is ground flat and worked through progressively finer grits — 80 all the way to 3000 — until the resin is glass-smooth and the wood grain sings. A food-safe matte or satin finish seals the piece.

04

Yours, Forever

Every table leaves Kanpur with a personal inspection and ships pan-India with full protective packaging. No two pieces are ever identical — the grain, the resin flow, the light it catches. That is the signature you live with.

What We Stand For

Three Commitments

Handcrafted Quality

Every piece leaves our Kanpur workshop with individual attention. We don’t operate on production lines — each table is a commission with its own grain story. No two are alike. Imperfection is the signature of the hand.

Sustainable Sourcing

We work with sustainably harvested hardwoods and low-VOC epoxy resins formulated to last generations. Our aim is furniture that outlives trends and outlasts landfill logic — built right, built once.

Custom-First Approach

“Uniquely crafted, just like you.” Every commission is a conversation — we design around your space, your preferences, your life. Our configurator starts that dialogue; our workshop finishes it by hand.
