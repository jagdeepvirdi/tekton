Our Story

Where Ancient Craft Meets Living Design

Tekton India was born in Kanpur out of a single conviction: that furniture should be made to outlast trends. We work with locally sourced mango wood — a timber with centuries of history in Indian craftsmanship — and pair it with precision-poured resin to create pieces that are simultaneously ancient and utterly contemporary.

Every table leaves our workshop with a unique serial number. No two are alike. The knots, the grain, the subtle shift in resin colour as the light moves — these are not imperfections. They are the signature of something made by human hands.
